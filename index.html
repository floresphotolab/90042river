Vocation

Photography (“to paint with light”) is my vocation. My whole humanity is occupied when I chisel the gradient of light, define the depth of field, compose the frame and record the miraculous. My soul is nourished when a client “sees” themselves for the first time – recognizes their youthfulness in old age or becomes aware of a microexpression or personality they didn’t know existed within themselves. The root word of respect is respicere which means “to look at.” When the client sees themselves, they respect themselves, this nourishes me by allowing the unique love I possess to be put to practice. 

Every tree is known by its own fruit. I have gone through many iterations of failure to imperfectly claim this as an orientation toward living life. I hope my service toward you bears fruit by providing a medium for you to verbalize the beauty that you see in life, others and yourself.
