About  


Ethics of Photography

Photography is a work of love. I seek to render your photographic experience accountable to the highest technical standard in the field at a price exemplary of a love of neighbor. I want to serve you and I want you to keep the photo, look back at it in years to come, and experience the existence that photograph captured at that moment in time.




Equipment

My camera of choice is a Sony A7RV. For event photography, I also use a Sony A7II. My lenses are a 55mm f/1.8, 70-200mm f/2.8, and 16-35 mm f/2.8, a prime lens for events, a telephoto lens for pet photography, events, and portraits, and a wide angle for dramatic pictures, respectively. A Tascam DR60D-MKII for audio capture. I use Godox, Flashpoint and Alien Bee as light sources. For onsite printing, I use a DS40 capable of 2x6 prints. Editing software is Photoshop, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Illustrator.




Purchasing Timeline

  • Contact me via email or phone to clarify the service rate, date, setting (outdoors or indoors), and your vision
  • Pre-production process begins, custom film strip & backdrop design for photobooth, research of venue for events, prepare shot list for portraits 
  • Production: photobooth setup an hour before listed time, portrait session lasts a minimum of 40 minutes
  • Post-Production: Lightroom and photoshop edits, turnaround time could be same day. Recieve an email or text of completion with an invoice and Google drive folder of photos. Hand deliver flashdrive, photo album or 4x6 prints.
